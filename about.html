The story

Second Space was founded in 2019 after a young womans thrift trip to Riga, Latvia.
A "second hand junkie" personality combined with a longing for making her own pieces of clothes
was what was the starting point of the dream about bringing her own brand to life.

Altered jackets with its colorful touch is what repersents the brand. Made out of reused fabric and
pieces of clothes that someone's left behind – Second Space give dull jackts a new funnier life.
